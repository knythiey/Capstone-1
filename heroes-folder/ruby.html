Lore

A long time ago, a brave hunter saved Little Red Riding Hood and her granny from a cunning wolf's belly and they lived happily ever after... though that was not how the real story ended. The wolf pack, harboring bitter resentment from this great shame, never forgot the scars on their bellies. One dark night when the moon was hidden, the new wolf king Haas led the pack in attacking the village, and the elderly hunter was slain in an attempt to save granny once more.
